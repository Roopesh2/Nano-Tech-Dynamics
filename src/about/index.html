About Us

Nano Tech Dynamics is the off-shoot partnership firm registered in the state of Kerala by three dynamic woman entrepreneurs and we have been allotted required land at Integrated Industrial defence park Lakkidi, Ottapalam, Kerala by KINFRA.

LN CNC Consultants

We started Computerized Numeric Control (CNC) operator and programmer training for Indian Industry way back in 1995. Back in those days small scale/private industries in Mumbai had very few CNC Machines operational. The field was starving for trained manpower to cater to the need of industries. As the need of industry developed we added CAD/CAM/CMM training into our portfolio among other designing/manufacturing processes. We expertise in all types of CNC controls and machines up to 11 axes. We are also the pioneers in Micro components suited for medical industry and biological micro reactors. Over the years we have also worked a lot in supporting heavy to very heavy industry catering to Ship and Submarine manufacturing as well. There was a huge demand in the ultra-precision component manufacturing industry for components and parts for Brayton / Stirling cryocoolers, Turbo expanders for helium liquefaction plants, Medical equipment accelerators among others. We identified this opportunity and stepped up to cater to the pressure of delivering high quality products for the above mentioned projects with our experience in the industry to the utmost satisfaction of our customers. We are extremely proud to announce that LN CNC Consultants were advisors for Diamond turning facility when it initiated for the first time in India. We also support IIT Bombay as Machining Consultant for Aero Space incubator set up. We have manufactured and supplied enormous Ultra precision component for Nuclear Industry as well. We also associated with Godrej Industries for Brahmos Project for the Defense Department. Among other works, we are proud to be one of the machining consultants for manufacturing Naval Ships and Sub-marine Mechanical Seals. We are expert in Machining of Memory Recognizing materials of Seal Rings for Tejas Air Craft. We have indigenously developed 4-Axis CNC Milling Machine for Micro Machining and Nano finishing. We are associated with surface finishing process and machine development for Metal Mirror polishing. Laser marking, ultra sound machining, electro chemical machining for Micro components manufacturing industry, supporting for ophthalmic lenses manufacturing is something we have worked extensively in.

Nano Tech Dynamics

Nano Tech Dynamics is an offshoot company of LNC CNC Consultants registered in the state of Kerala aiming to contribute the manufacturing of ultra-precision components sector. We have been attracted by the present Kerala Government's policy to invite investments/startups to begin new endeavors for industrial development and employment generation. We have observed that in the area of cutting edge technology in the private sector, the state is lagging behind and our expertise can be enhanced with more exposure. We are proposing to commence a venture in the Kinfra Defence ParkOttapalam and The KINFRA is allotted required land for setting up an industry in the above mentioned model.
